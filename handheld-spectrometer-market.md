<p><p>Handheld Spectrometer market consists of two main types - Optical Spectrometer and Electromagnetic Spectrometer. Optical Spectrometers analyze light in the visible, ultraviolet, and infrared regions to identify the chemical composition of materials. On the other hand, Electromagnetic Spectrometers measure the electromagnetic radiation emitted or absorbed by substances to determine their properties and composition. Both types of spectrometers are portable, user-friendly devices that provide accurate and reliable results for various applications including environmental monitoring, pharmaceuticals, and food safety.</p></p>

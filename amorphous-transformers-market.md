<p><p>Amorphous transformers are an energy-efficient option for power distribution, with two main types: Oil-Immersed and Dry-Type. Oil-Immersed transformers use oil as a cooling agent and insulator, making them suitable for high power applications. Dry-Type transformers, on the other hand, use air or solid insulation, making them safer for indoor use and requiring less maintenance. Both types are known for their compact design, low noise operation, and high efficiency in transferring electrical energy. Overall, Amorphous Transformers are a reliable and cost-effective choice for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>

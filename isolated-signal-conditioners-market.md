<p><p>Isolated signal conditioners are devices that separate signal inputs from outputs, providing electrical isolation to protect sensitive equipment. The market for isolated signal conditioners is segmented into three types based on the number of channels they support: single channel, dual channel, and multi-channel. Single channel signal conditioners allow for isolation of one input/output signal, dual channel signal conditioners offer isolation for two signals, and multi-channel signal conditioners provide isolation for multiple signals simultaneously. Each type caters to different user needs and application requirements.</p></p>

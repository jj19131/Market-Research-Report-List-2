<p><strong>The Range Finder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser</li><li>Ultrasonic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Range Finder Market comprises of two main types - Laser and Ultrasonic. Laser Range Finders use a laser beam to measure the distance between the device and the target accurately. These devices are often used in applications where precision is crucial, such as surveying and construction. On the other hand, Ultrasonic Range Finders use sound waves to determine the distance between the device and the target. They are commonly used in industrial settings, robotics, and automotive applications due to their ability to work in various environmental conditions.</p></p>

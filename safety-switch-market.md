<p><p>Safety switch market is categorized into two types: contact switch and non-contact switch. Contact switches require physical contact or pressure to operate, such as a push-button switch. Non-contact switches, on the other hand, use proximity or magnetic fields to function without direct physical contact, such as a motion sensor. Both types of switches are designed to ensure safety by controlling power flow and preventing accidents in various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>

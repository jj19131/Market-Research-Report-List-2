<p><ul><li>Cooling Dehumidifier</li><li>Rotary Dehumidifier</li><li>Pipeline Dehumidifier</li><li>Electroosmosis Dehumidifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dehumidity unit market consists of four main types: Cooling Dehumidifier, Rotary Dehumidifier, Pipeline Dehumidifier, and Electroosmosis Dehumidifier. Cooling dehumidifiers use a cooling coil to condense moisture from the air, while rotary dehumidifiers use a rotating wheel to absorb moisture. Pipeline dehumidifiers are connected to a building's HVAC system to remove excess humidity, and electroosmosis dehumidifiers use an electrical field to extract moisture from the air. Each type of dehumidifier offers unique benefits and applications in managing indoor humidity levels.</p></p>

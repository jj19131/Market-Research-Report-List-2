<p><p>Micro Electromechanical Systems (MEMS) are small integrated devices that combine electrical and mechanical components on a microscopic scale. The market is classified into different types based on the applications they serve. Optical MEMS are used in devices like smartphones and projectors. Inkjet Head MEMS are found in inkjet printers for precise droplet formation. Microfluidics MEMS are used for managing small amounts of fluids in applications like medical devices. Radio Frequency (RF) MEMS are used in wireless communication devices for signal processing and tuning.</p></p>

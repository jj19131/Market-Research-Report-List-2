<p><p>Key operated switches are available in different types based on the number of positions they have. A 1-position key operated switch can only be turned on or off. A 2-position switch can have two settings, typically on and off. A 3-position switch adds an extra setting, usually for a momentary or temporary function. A 4-position switch has four possible settings, allowing for more versatile control options. These different types cater to a range of applications and user preferences.</p></p>

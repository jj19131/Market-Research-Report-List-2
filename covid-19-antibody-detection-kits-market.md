<p><strong>The COVID-19 Antibody Detection Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>COVID-19 antibody detection kits come in three main types: Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT), Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA), and Neutralization Assay. RDTs provide quick results by detecting antibodies in blood samples. ELISA tests use enzymes to detect antibodies and provide quantitative results. Neutralization assays measure the ability of antibodies to neutralize the virus. Each type of kit offers different advantages in terms of speed, accuracy, and ease of use, catering to the diverse needs of healthcare professionals and individuals.</p></p>

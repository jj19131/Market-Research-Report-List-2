<p><p>The spraying nozzles market includes various types, with two main categories being pressure nozzles and airflow nozzles. Pressure nozzles are designed to deliver a precise stream of liquid at a high pressure, making them ideal for applications requiring accurate and controlled spraying. On the other hand, airflow nozzles use compressed air to create a fine mist of liquid, providing a more gentle and versatile spraying option. Both types cater to different needs and are widely used in industries such as agriculture, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>

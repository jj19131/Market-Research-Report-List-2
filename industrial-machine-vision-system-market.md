<p><strong>The Industrial Machine Vision System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PC-Based Vision System</li><li>Smart Camera-Based Vision System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Machine Vision System market can be categorized into two main types: PC-Based Vision System and Smart Camera-Based Vision System. PC-Based Vision Systems utilize high-performance computers to process and analyze images captured by industrial cameras, providing advanced image processing capabilities. On the other hand, Smart Camera-Based Vision Systems integrate image capture, processing, and analysis within a single compact unit, offering a more streamlined and cost-effective solution for certain applications. Both systems play a crucial role in automation and quality control processes in various industries.</p></p>

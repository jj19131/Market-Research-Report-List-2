<p><strong>The Direct-Coat IR Glazing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monolayer</li><li>Multilayer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct-coat IR glazing is available in two main types: monolayer and multilayer. Monolayer glazing consists of a single layer of coating applied directly to the surface of the glass, providing basic infrared protection. Multilayer glazing, on the other hand, involves multiple layers of coating, offering enhanced infrared blocking capabilities. Monolayer glazing is typically more cost-effective, while multilayer glazing provides superior performance and is often used in high-end applications where maximum infrared protection is desired.</p></p>

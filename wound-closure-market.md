<p><p>Wound closure market is segmented into four main types: sutures, hemostatic agents, staplers, and other closure devices. Sutures are threads used to sew tissue together. Hemostatic agents help control bleeding during surgical procedures. Staplers are devices used to quickly and efficiently close wounds by firing metal staples into the skin. The "other" category includes devices such as adhesives, tapes, and sealants used for wound closure. Each type of wound closure product offers unique benefits and is suited for different types of wounds and procedures.</p></p>

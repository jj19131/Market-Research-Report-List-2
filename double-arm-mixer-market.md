<p><p>Double arm mixers are available in two main types on the market: Open type and Enclosed type. Open type mixers are designed with exposed arms and bowls, allowing for easy monitoring and cleaning during operation. This type is commonly used in smaller scale production environments. Enclosed type mixers, on the other hand, have a closed design with protective covers around the arms and bowl. This type is suitable for larger scale operations where sanitation and containment are critical. Both types offer efficient mixing capabilities but cater to different production needs.</p></p>

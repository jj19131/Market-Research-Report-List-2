<p><p>Flexible electrical conduits are classified into two main types: metal and non-metal. Metal conduits, such as steel or aluminum, provide superior protection against physical damage and high temperatures. They are often used in industrial and commercial applications where durability is crucial. Non-metal conduits, on the other hand, are typically made of materials like PVC or nylon and are lightweight and easy to install. They are commonly used in residential settings where flexibility and cost-effectiveness are important factors. Both types of conduits play a vital role in safely routing and protecting electrical wiring.</p></p>

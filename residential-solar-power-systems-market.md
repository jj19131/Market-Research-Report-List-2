<p><p>Off-grid solar power systems are designed for properties that are not connected to the main electricity grid. Off-grid with Grid Support systems incorporate a backup generator or alternative power source for additional support. Grid-tied systems allow properties to generate their own electricity while remaining connected to the grid. Grid-tied with Battery Back Up systems store excess energy in batteries for use during power outages or when grid electricity prices are high. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences of residential solar power users.</p></p>

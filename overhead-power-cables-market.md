<p><p>Overhead Power Cables are classified into three main types based on voltage levels - High Voltage, Medium Voltage, and Low Voltage markets. High Voltage cables typically operate at voltages above 100 kV and are used for long distance transmission of electricity. Medium Voltage cables operate between 1 kV and 69 kV, serving to distribute power within local areas. Low Voltage cables, with voltages below 1 kV, are commonly used for supplying electricity to residential and commercial properties within shorter distances.</p></p>

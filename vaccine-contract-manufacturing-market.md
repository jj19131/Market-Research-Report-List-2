<p><p>Vaccine contract manufacturing market includes two main types: fill-finish and bulk product. Fill-finish refers to the final stage of vaccine production where the product is filled into vials or syringes, labeled, and packaged for distribution. Bulk product market involves the production of the actual vaccine substance in large quantities before it is filled and finished. Both types play a crucial role in the vaccine manufacturing process, providing essential services for pharmaceutical companies to efficiently produce and distribute vaccines.</p></p>

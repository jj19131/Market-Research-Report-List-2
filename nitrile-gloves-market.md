<p><ul><li>Powder-Free Nitrile Gloves</li><li>Powdered Nitrile Gloves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nitrile gloves market can be categorized into two main types: Powder-Free Nitrile Gloves and Powdered Nitrile Gloves. Powder-Free Nitrile Gloves are more commonly used as they reduce the risk of allergic reactions and contamination associated with glove powder. They are also preferred in industries where powder residue is undesirable. On the other hand, Powdered Nitrile Gloves are easier to put on and take off due to the powder coating. However, they are not as commonly used as powder-free gloves due to concerns over powder residue.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Industrial Overload Relays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Overload Relays</li><li>Magnetic Overload Relays</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial overload relays market is segmented into three main types - thermal overload relays, magnetic overload relays, and others. Thermal overload relays work by sensing the heat generated by overcurrent in the motor, while magnetic overload relays operate by detecting the magnetic field generated by overcurrent. The others category includes electronic overload relays, which use electronic components to sense and respond to overcurrent conditions. Each type of overload relay provides protection against motor damage and ensures safe operation in industrial applications.</p></p>

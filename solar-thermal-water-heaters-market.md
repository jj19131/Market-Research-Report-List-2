<p><ul><li>Pressure-bearing Solar Water Heater</li><li>Non-pressure Solar Water Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solar Thermal Water Heaters market offers two main types of products: Pressure-bearing Solar Water Heater and Non-pressure Solar Water Heater. Pressure-bearing systems use a pump to circulate water through the solar collector and into the storage tank under pressure, suitable for cold climates. Non-pressure systems rely on gravity to circulate water, making them more suitable for warm climates. Both types utilize the sun's energy to heat water for various residential and commercial applications, offering environmentally friendly and cost-effective heating solutions.</p></p>

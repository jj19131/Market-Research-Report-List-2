Glass fiber composite tanks are lightweight and cost-effective, making them a popular choice for CNG storage. Carbon fiber composite tanks offer higher strength-to-weight ratio and better performance but come at a higher cost. Metal tanks are durable and commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles.

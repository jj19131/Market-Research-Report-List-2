<p><p>Rechargeable power tools are available in three main market types: Nickle Cadmium (NiCd), Nickle Metal Hydride (NiMH), and Lithium Ion (Li-Ion). NiCd batteries are known for their durability and resistance to extreme temperatures, while NiMH batteries offer higher energy density and longer lifespan. Li-Ion batteries are lighter, have higher energy density, and longer lifespan compared to NiCd and NiMH batteries. There are other emerging technologies in the market, such as Lithium Polymer (Li-Po) batteries, which offer even higher energy density and longer lifespan.</p></p>

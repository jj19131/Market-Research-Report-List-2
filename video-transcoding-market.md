H.265 offers better compression while maintaining quality, ideal for streaming high-quality videos. VP9 is a Google-developed format focusing on delivering high-quality videos with improved bandwidth utilization. AV1 is a cutting-edge format offering even better compression and quality, but requires more processing power. Each format serves different needs in the video transcoding market.</p></p>

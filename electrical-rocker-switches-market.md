<p><p>Electrical rocker switches come in various types to suit different applications. SPST rocker switches have a single pole, single throw design ideal for simple on/off functions. SPDT rocker switches have a single pole, double throw design allowing for control of two circuits. DPST rocker switches feature a double pole, single throw configuration suitable for controlling two separate circuits simultaneously. DPDT rocker switches have a double pole, double throw design allowing for even more circuit control options. Each type serves specific purposes based on the desired functionality.</p></p>

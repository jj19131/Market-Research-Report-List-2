<p><ul><li>Steel sucker rod</li><li>FRP sucker rod</li><li>Hollow sucker rod</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sucker rod market consists of three main types: steel, FRP, and hollow. Steel sucker rods are widely used in the oil and gas industry due to their strength and durability. FRP (fiberglass reinforced plastic) sucker rods are lightweight and corrosion-resistant, making them ideal for challenging environments. Hollow sucker rods are designed to reduce the overall weight of the rod while maintaining strength, making them suitable for deep well applications. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the operation.</p></p>

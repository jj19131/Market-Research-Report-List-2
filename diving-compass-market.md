<p><p>The diving compass market consists of two main types: digital compasses and pointer compasses. Digital compasses utilize electronic sensors to provide accurate direction readings, often displaying them on a screen for easy viewing. Pointer compasses, on the other hand, use a magnetic needle that points towards magnetic north. Both types are popular choices among divers for navigating underwater environments. Digital compasses offer more advanced features, while pointer compasses are favored for their simplicity and reliability.</p></p>

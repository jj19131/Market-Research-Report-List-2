<p><strong>The Ventilation Boxes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ventilation boxes market can be categorized into two types: Active and Passive. </p><p>Active ventilation boxes are equipped with mechanical systems such as fans or blowers to regulate air flow and ventilation in buildings. These systems require electrical power to operate and are generally more efficient in maintaining indoor air quality.</p><p>Passive ventilation boxes, on the other hand, rely on natural airflow and convection to provide ventilation. These systems do not require electrical power and are more sustainable and cost-effective in the long run. Both types serve the purpose of maintaining healthy indoor air quality but differ in their approach to achieving it.</p></p>

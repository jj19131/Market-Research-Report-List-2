<p><p>Ionizer blowers are typically categorized into two main market types: Stand Blower and Overhead Blower. Stand blowers are designed to be placed on a surface or stand, providing targeted ionization for specific areas. On the other hand, overhead blowers are installed on ceilings or overhead structures to provide ionization coverage over a larger area. Both types of ionizer blowers are used to neutralize static charges, reduce dust accumulation, and improve overall air quality in various industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>

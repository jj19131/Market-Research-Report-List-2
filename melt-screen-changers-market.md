<p><p>Melt screen changers are used in plastic processing equipment to ensure a continuous flow of material by removing contaminants and impurities. There are four main types of melt screen changers: continuous, semi-continuous, discontinuous, and others. </p><p>Continuous screen changers operate without any interruption in the flow of material, providing a seamless process. Semi-continuous screen changers allow for occasional interruptions to change screens. Discontinuous screen changers require frequent interruptions for screen changes. Other types of screen changers may have unique features or functionalities that differentiate them from the traditional types.</p></p>

<p><p>Voltage Monitoring Relays come in two main types - Single-Phase and Three-Phase. </p><p>Single-Phase relays are designed to monitor the voltage of a single line, commonly used in residential and small commercial applications. They provide protection against voltage deviations and abnormalities to prevent damages to electrical equipment.</p><p>Three-Phase relays, on the other hand, are designed to monitor the voltage of three interconnected lines in larger commercial and industrial settings. They offer advanced monitoring and control capabilities to ensure stable and reliable power distribution.</p></p>

<p><p>Terminal connectors are devices used to connect wires or cables to electrical equipment. The market for terminal connectors can be categorized into various types including Screw Type, Spring-cage Type, Push-in Type, and Others. Screw Type connectors utilize screws to secure wires in place, while Spring-cage Type connectors use a spring mechanism for easy insertion and removal of wires. Push-in Type connectors require wires to be inserted directly into the terminal. Other types of terminal connectors include insulation displacement, barrier strip, and blade connectors.</p></p>

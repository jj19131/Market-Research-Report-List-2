<p><ul><li>AlN Heater</li><li>Alumina Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wafer heaters come in two main types - AlN heater and Alumina heater. AlN heaters are made of aluminum nitride material known for its high thermal conductivity and excellent dielectric properties, making it suitable for high temperature applications. On the other hand, Alumina heaters are made of aluminum oxide material which is also known for its high thermal conductivity and heat resistance. Both types of heaters are widely used in various industries for heating applications in semiconductor processing, research labs, and electronics manufacturing.</p></p>

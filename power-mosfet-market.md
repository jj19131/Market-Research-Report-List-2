<p><p>Power MOSFET market is divided into two main types - Depletion Mode Power MOSFET, and Enhancement Mode Power MOSFET. Depletion Mode Power MOSFETs are normally ON devices that require a negative voltage to turn off, while Enhancement Mode Power MOSFETs are typically OFF devices that require a positive voltage to turn on. Both types cater to different applications and industries, offering unique features and benefits based on their operational modes. Businesses can choose between these two types based on their specific requirements and performance criteria.</p></p>

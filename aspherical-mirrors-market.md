<p><ul><li>Toroidal Mirrors</li><li>Off-axis Parabolic Mirrors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aspherical mirrors come in various types, including Toroidal Mirrors and Off-axis Parabolic Mirrors. Toroidal mirrors have a unique shape that helps reduce spherical aberrations, making them ideal for applications requiring high image quality. On the other hand, Off-axis Parabolic Mirrors are designed to eliminate off-axis aberrations, allowing for precise imaging and focusing of light beams. Both types of mirrors are commonly used in optical systems such as telescopes, cameras, and laser systems for various scientific and industrial applications.</p></p>

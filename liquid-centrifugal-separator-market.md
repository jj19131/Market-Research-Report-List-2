<p><p>Filtration Centrifuges are used for separating solid particles from a liquid by passing it through a perforated filter, allowing only the liquid to pass through. Sedimentation Centrifuges work by allowing solid particles to settle at the bottom of a rotating container, separating them from the liquid. Both types of centrifuges are commonly used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and wastewater treatment to separate components based on their density or particle size.</p></p>

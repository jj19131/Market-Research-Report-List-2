<p><p>Pasteurizers are available in three market types: Automatic, Semi-automatic, and Manual. </p><p>Automatic pasteurizers are fully automated machines that require minimal human intervention, making them ideal for large-scale production. Semi-automatic pasteurizers require some human input for loading and unloading but largely operate on their own. Manual pasteurizers are operated entirely by hand, making them suitable for smaller scale operations or artisanal producers. Each market type offers a different level of automation and can be chosen based on the production scale and budget of the customer.</p></p>

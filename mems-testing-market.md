<p><p>MEMS Testing market includes various types of testing methods to ensure the accuracy and reliability of MEMS devices. Kelvin Testing is a method used to measure the resistance of a device by passing a current through it and measuring the voltage drop. Four-probe Testing, on the other hand, involves using four probes to measure resistivity and conductivity of materials with high accuracy. Both these methods are crucial in evaluating the performance and quality of MEMS devices in different applications.</p></p>

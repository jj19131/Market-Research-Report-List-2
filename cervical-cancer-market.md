<p><p>Cervical cancer market types include Pap testing, HPV testing, colposcopy, and cervical biopsies. Pap testing involves examining cells from the cervix for abnormalities. HPV testing detects the presence of high-risk strains of the virus that can lead to cancer. Colposcopy is a procedure to closely examine the cervix for signs of disease. Cervical biopsies involve removing a small sample of tissue from the cervix to diagnose abnormalities. These tests and procedures are crucial for early detection and treatment of cervical cancer.</p></p>

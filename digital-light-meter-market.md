<p><strong>The Digital Light Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflected-Light</li><li>Incident-Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Light Meter market is divided into two main types: Reflected-Light and Incident-Light meters. Reflected-Light meters measure the light that is being reflected off a subject, which is commonly used in photography and cinematography to ensure proper exposure. Incident-Light meters, on the other hand, measure the amount of light falling on a subject, providing accurate readings regardless of the subject's reflectivity. Both types of meters are essential tools for photographers, videographers, and professionals working in lighting-intensive industries.</p></p>

<p><p>Commercial HVAC chillers are essential for controlling temperatures in large buildings. There are two main types of chillers in the market: air-cooling and water-cooling. Air-cooling chillers use air to remove heat from the refrigerant, making them more cost-effective and easier to install. On the other hand, water-cooling chillers use water as a coolant, offering higher efficiency and better performance in larger commercial spaces. Both types have their advantages and are used in various industries depending on the specific needs of the building.</p></p>

<p><p>Passive Terahertz Imaging in the industrial market refers to the use of Terahertz technology to passively detect and image objects by measuring the natural radiation emitted by them. On the other hand, Active Terahertz Imaging involves the use of Terahertz waves that are actively emitted and reflected off objects to create an image. Both types of Terahertz imaging have applications in industrial processes such as quality control, material inspection, and security screening due to their ability to penetrate various materials and provide detailed imaging.</p></p>

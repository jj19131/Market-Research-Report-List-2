<p><p>Plate Frame Heat Exchangers are commonly available in two main types - Gasketed Plate and Frame Heat Exchangers, and Welded Plate and Frame Heat Exchangers. Gasketed Plate and Frame Heat Exchangers use gaskets to seal the plates together, allowing for easier maintenance and cleaning. Welded Plate and Frame Heat Exchangers, on the other hand, are sealed using welding, providing a more durable and secure solution. Both types offer efficient heat transfer capabilities and are widely used in various industries for cooling and heating applications.</p></p>

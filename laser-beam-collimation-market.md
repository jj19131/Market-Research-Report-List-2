<p><strong>The Laser Beam Collimation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective Collimators</li><li>Refractive Collimators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser Beam Collimation market consists of two main types: Reflective Collimators and Refractive Collimators. Reflective Collimators use mirrors to reflect and align laser beams, offering high precision and minimal beam distortion. They are commonly used in applications requiring high power and long-distance beam delivery. Refractive Collimators, on the other hand, use lenses to refract and align laser beams, providing versatility and ease of use. They are popular in applications where compact size and low cost are essential.</p></p>

<p><p>Boat radar reflectors are essential for improving the visibility of boats and enhancing safety on the water. There are two main types of boat radar reflectors: Octahedral and Luneberg Lens. Octahedral reflectors have a simple geometric shape that provides 360-degree visibility, making them ideal for smaller boats. Luneberg Lens reflectors are more advanced, with a spherical lens design that offers enhanced radar cross-section. These reflectors are commonly used on larger vessels where maximum visibility is crucial for avoiding collisions.</p></p>

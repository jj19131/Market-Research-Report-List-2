<p><p>Torque limiting clutches are used in various industrial applications to protect machinery from damage caused by excessive torque. The market for these clutches can be categorized into three types: friction type, ball type, and others. Friction type clutches rely on the friction between two surfaces to limit torque, while ball type clutches use a mechanism with steel balls to limit torque. The "others" category includes clutches with different mechanisms, such as magnetic or overload clutches. Each type of torque limiting clutch offers unique advantages and is suited for specific applications.</p></p>

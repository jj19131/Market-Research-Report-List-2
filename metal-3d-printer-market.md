<p><strong>The Metal 3D Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Selective Laser Melting (SLM)</li><li>Electronic Beam Melting (EBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electronic Beam Melting (EBM) are two types of metal 3D printing technologies. SLM uses a high-powered laser to selectively melt and fuse metal powder together layer by layer, while EBM uses an electron beam to achieve a similar result. Both technologies offer high precision and are used in various industries like aerospace, automotive, and healthcare for prototyping, tooling, and production of complex metal parts. The market for SLM and EBM is growing rapidly due to their advantages in terms of design flexibility and material efficiency.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Floor Standing</li><li>Basic Rack</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Floor standing PDUs are designed to be placed on the floor or mounted to a wall, typically in larger data centers or industrial spaces. They offer higher power capacities and flexibility in configuration. Basic rack PDUs, on the other hand, are designed to be mounted within a server rack, providing power distribution specifically for rack-mounted equipment. These PDUs are more compact and lightweight, making them ideal for smaller data centers or office environments where space is limited. Both types offer basic power distribution without additional advanced features.</p></p>

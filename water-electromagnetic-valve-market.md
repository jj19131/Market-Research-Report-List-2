<p><p>Water Electromagnetic Valve market consists of various types including Straight Moving Type, Step By Step Straight Moving Type, and Pilot Operated Type. The Straight Moving Type valve opens and closes directly through electromagnetic force. The Step By Step Straight Moving Type valve operates in incremental steps ensuring precise control. The Pilot Operated Type valve uses a small pilot valve to control the main valve, allowing for higher flow rates and pressure capabilities. Each type offers unique advantages and is utilized in different applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>

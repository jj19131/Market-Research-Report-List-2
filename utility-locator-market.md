<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Field</li><li>Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Utility locator market is segmented into two main types: Electromagnetic Field and Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR). Electromagnetic Field utility locators are used to detect underground utilities by sending out electromagnetic signals and detecting the response from buried lines. On the other hand, Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) utility locators use radar pulses to image the subsurface and detect buried utilities. Both types of utility locators are essential tools for safely and accurately locating underground utilities before digging or construction projects.</p></p>

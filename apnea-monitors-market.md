<p><strong>The Apnea Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel Screening Devices</li><li>Actigraphy Systems</li><li>PSG Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Apnea monitors are used to detect and monitor breathing irregularities during sleep. There are three main types of apnea monitors on the market. Single channel screening devices are simple and cost-effective monitors that detect apnea events based on breathing patterns. Actigraphy systems use movement and activity levels to track sleep patterns and detect apnea events. PSG devices are comprehensive sleep monitoring systems that can diagnose a range of sleep disorders, including apnea. Each type of monitor offers a different level of functionality and accuracy in detecting and monitoring apnea events.</p></p>
